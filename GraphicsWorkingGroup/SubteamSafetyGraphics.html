Subteam Information Pages

Within the working group as a whole, it was decided that four subteams would be formed to address graphical needs within each topic area. Below is a listing of the four subteams and links to pages for each.

ECG/Vitals Subteam

The ECG/Vitals subteam is responsible for identifying visualizations of data related to vital signs and ECG. This subteam is lead by Richard Anziano.

General Adverse Events Subteam

The General Adverse Event Subteam is responsible for identifying ways to views general adverse event data, such as data collected using the MedDRA dictionary. This subteam is led by Stephine Keeton.

General Principles Subteam

The General Principles Subteam is responsible for several taks. Some of these are related to identifying good graphical principles and identifying templates for graphic presentation in a public domain. This subteam has been first led by Susan Duke, and now jointly led by Richard Forshee and Fabrice Bancken.

Labs/Liver Subteam

The Labs/Liver Subteam is responsible for developing graphical visualizations of laboratory data collected in clinical trials as well as data related to assessing liver function. This subteam is lead by Mac Gordon.
