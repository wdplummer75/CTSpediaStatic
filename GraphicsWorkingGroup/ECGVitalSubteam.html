Developments

The goal of this subgroup is to identify the clinical/medical questions and corresponding set of graphical displays that are most useful for evaluating safety data specific to ECG and Vital Signs. Our progress to date is noted in the attachments below.

Attendees: Liping Huang, Antonio Paredes, Rich Anziano

Meeting Summary:

The meeting for June was cancelled.

We had a very brief meeting.

Antonio indicated that he and Eric have been swamped and haven't made progress on producing and publishing the remaining plots to CTSPEDIA. Antonio will send a project timetable as this is the last outstanding item for our subgroup.

We discussed the email string about the possibility of our Industry/FDA/Academia reforming under DIA. It was generally felt that the benefits of having better exposure for our graphic tools would be outweighed by the potential loss of our academic partners who may not be members of DIA.
