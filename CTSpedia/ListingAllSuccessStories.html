Directions for Approving Your Success Stories

  1. Click on "edit" following SuccessStoriesForm
  2. Title: Provide a title for your success story and take off parenthesis that I used to indicate a made up title.
  3. Year: Mark years that the success story covers.
  4. Contributor/Contact: Provide name and email (if you wish to be contacted by others).
  5. Description of Success: Review description of your activities and add more information..
  6. Open to Public: Mark Yes if you want the public to see what is currently written, No if you do not wish the public to see what is written. (Once you mark "Yes," it go in the public listings.)
  7. Expand Description: Mark Expand if you wish to expand the description before opening this page to the public.
  8. BERD Watch: Mark Yes if you would like to expand this success story into a 300 word BERD Watch article.
  9. Suggestions: Please provide any suggestions for improving this section.
