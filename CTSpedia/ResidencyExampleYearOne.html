Residency Project - Year One

In the first year of your research project you will want to formalize your question of interest, determine the feasibility of your study, and write and submit an Institutional Review Board (IRB) proposal.

This example will follow two residents through their first year of their residency project. Paul is conducting a retrospective cohort study while Margo is conducting a retrospective case-control study.
