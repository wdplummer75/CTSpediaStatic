To Add a Comment on any of the Case Studies

Please feel free to add a new biostatistics case study or comment on any of the case studies:
  • TO CONTRIBUTE to an ongoing discussion of a case study, click on title to open the discussion and add your comments. (You need to be registered.) Be sure to save your comments when complete. Thanks!

To Contribute a New Case Study using the Form

  • NEW Case Study: Uploaded by You
  • Click on the "Create Biostat Case Study ###" button, a new topic page will open. (You need to be registered.)
  • Put the title at the top of the page.
  • Fill out the form on the bottom of the page.
  • Material must be de-identified and must not have any copyright restrictions
  • Save the page.
