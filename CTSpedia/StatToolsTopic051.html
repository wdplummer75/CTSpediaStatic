Type of Tool SAS Macro
Title ZIP GEE
Programmer/Email Rui Chen
Contributing Site Rochester
Description Rochester_GEEZIP is a macro that models overdispersion with structural zero data within a longitudinal data setting. The macro uses a distribution-free approach through generalized estimating equations to estimate effects of continuous covariates developed by Yu et al (2010). The asymptotic variances for parameter estimates are also computed. It\x92s suggested to be used in conjunction with ZIP Check Fit macro, which examines whether preponderance of zeros of count data is present (see details Rochester_ZIP_Check_Fit).
