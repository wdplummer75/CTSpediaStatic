SAS Community

http://www.sascommunity.org/

sasCommunity.org is a collaborative online SAS Community for SAS Users worldwide and was created by the SAS Global Users Group Executive Board. sasCommunity.org is the single entry point that any SAS user world-wide can go to in order to find any/all resources that might be of value to them in their use of SAS software and its related technologies.

Sasopedia

http://www.sascommunity.org/wiki/Sasopedia

Sasopedia is a place where users of SAS can provide factual information and tips on using SAS software. There are various ways of organizing the information.

  • by Procedure
  • by Product/Solution
  • by SAS Statement
  • by Topic

Categories are used to organize this information as discussed in Organizing Articles which is also found in the getting started box.

The Sasopedia is intended to serve the needs of what many people refer to as a FAQ ( F requently A sked Q uestions).
