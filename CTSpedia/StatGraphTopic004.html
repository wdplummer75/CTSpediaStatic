Title Depicting Phase 2 Efficacy Results
Graph_Subgroup General Principles
Code Added Yes
Description Scatterplot of the baseline and end of treatment lesion counts with super-imposed smoothed regression lines. In addition, tabular information is provided near the left-hand side of the graphic to give information about the magnitude of the overall treatment effect.
Contributor/Email Mat Soukup (email: Mat.Soukup@fda.hhs.gov)
Background This is a simulated example where the treatment effect (fewer lesions imply greater health benefit) is to be most pronounced in subjects with a low number of baseline lesions.
