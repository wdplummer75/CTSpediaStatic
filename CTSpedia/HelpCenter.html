Our public space is open to all interested parties. We would prefer if you hyperlinked to our materials, always including the CTSpedia URL, rather than downloading the files, particularly PDFs and slides. Once the files are copied to your desktop or your site we do not have any way of knowing who is using our materials. Please acknowledge CTSpedia whenever you use or refer to our materials.

Registration and Getting Acquainted

In order to contribute to our site, you must register. This is a manual process. Please allow 24 hours for the registration. The administrator must be notified of the registration and then manually enter you into the appropriate groups. When you register, you will also see a short tutorial on how the wiki works.
