Instructions for editing Controversies

You can edit, paste, or type text on one side or the other of an existing point by clicking the "Edit" button below the table. (To include formatting or special characters, however, you may need to instead follow the instructions below.)

To edit the overall background or the introduction for a particular point, click the Edit button at the upper right of the page. After logging in (if needed), you can then scroll to where you want to edit and add or modify text as for any CTSpedia page.

To add a new point, go to the template page and follow the instructions there.
