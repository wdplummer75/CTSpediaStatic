Add a new reference:

Adding References:
  1. Click on "Add CTSpedia Reference" button.
  2. Page opens. Add information.
  3. If the article will open without going through a proxy server, put in URL.
  4. If the article needs to go through proxy server, e.g. DOI, add DOI.
  5. Check appropriate subject/s in Reference_Subject box.
  6. Save (on bottom of page).
