Lecture Series - Science of Small Clinical Trials

To access these lectures, follow these steps:

1) Go to http://videocast.nih.gov/

2) Select “Past Events” from the menu on the left

3) Select “Search” from the menu bar along the top

4) Search for small clinical trials selecting for "Include all Words"
