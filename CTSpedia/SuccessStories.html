Creating Success Stories

  1. Click on "Create Success Story###" button.
  2. Please add information directly into the form at the bottom of the page and save. (Add any attachments to the page by clicking on Attach and uploading documents.)
  3. Title: Provide a title for your success story.
  4. Year: Mark years that the success story covers.
  5. Contributor/Contact: Provide name and email (if you wish to be contacted by others).
  6. Mark your CTSA.
  7. Success Topic: Choose Success Topic(s).
  8. Description of Success: Provide description of your activities.
  9. Open to Public: Mark Yes if you want the public to see what is currently written, No if you do not wish the public to see what is written.
  10. Expand Description: Mark Expand if you wish to expand the description before opening this page to the public.
  11. BERD Watch: Mark Yes if you would like to expand this success story into a 300 word BERD Watch article.
  12. Suggestions: Please provide any suggestions for improving this section.
