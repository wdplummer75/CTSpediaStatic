Instructions for Submitting Course Materials:
  • Click on the "Add Educational Materials" button. Complete the form that opens on the page.
  • Add the title on top of page and inside the form box. Start the title with your Institution's Name and a dash, e.g. Institution - Introduction to Clinical Trials. (Please keep your titles short - eight or less words.)
  • If you have datasets that you are using for your course, please attach them and add the link to the box marked "Data." (If you need a dataset for your course, please check our shared datasets.
  • If you have additional references or materials for your course, please add the URL or attach the document linking them in the "See Also" box.
  • Attaching your materials - follow attachments and linking instructions.
