The name you log in with

Your username is a sequence of alphanumeric characters and underscores that uniquely identifies you to the system. The exact format of the username depends upon how your system is configured.

This system is not configured to use a distinct username. You will log in using your WikiName.

Your Wikiname is WikiAdmin and your username is WikiAdmin.

You cannot alter your username: you have to get the administrator to do this for you.
